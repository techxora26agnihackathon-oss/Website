About TECHXORA '26

TECHXORA '26 is a 24-hour national-level hackathon organized by the Department of Computer Science & Engineering, Agni College of Technology, Chennai. It brings together the brightest student minds to ideate, build, and demo functional solutions to real-world challenges.

Whether you're a seasoned full-stack developer or just starting your journey – TECHXORA is your stage. Collaborate with young minds, get mentored by experts, and compete for cash prizes.
